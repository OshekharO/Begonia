Installation Guide Android R

Upgrade to Custom RoM from COS

  • Flash SHRP Recovery.
  • Navigate to WIPE>ADVANCED WIPE.
  • Select System, Cache, Data, Internal Storage & Vendor and wipe.
  • Now transfer C20 OZIP to your internal storage.
    (via pc or OTG or SD CARD)
  • Navigate to Install, select you OZIP and flash.
  • Navigate to WIPE>ADVANCED WIPE.
  • Select System, Cache, Data & Internal Storage and wipe.
    (Warning: Don't Wipe Vendor)
  • Flash SHRP Recovery.
    (because when you flash OZIP, stock recovery gets installed)
  • Mount everything.
  • Flash Custom RoM.
  • Flash Keystore_Patcher.
  • Flash TWRP_Decrypt.
  • Navigate to WIPE>ADVANCED WIPE> Select DATA and Wipe
    (repeat 12 & 13, 2-3 times)
  • Flash Modified_Boot.
  • Flash Magisk 21.4.
  • Flash GApps.
